Dean Pektas

Dean Pektas is a student at Queens College and majors in computer science. Throughout his time outside of class, he joined Codepath and interned at Numeraxial to learn how to develop applications for multiple web and mobile platforms. Dean has and continues to take what he has learned to provide the best web platform that drives Diverse Influencers as the Director of Web Development. Beyond work, he also enjoys discovering new places to visit with his friends.

Nick Guillen

Nick is a first-generation college student pursuing an MS in Human Resources at CUNY Lehman College. Previously, Nick has completed internships at PwC and JPMorgan Chase & Co. He will be returning to JPMorgan as an Analyst in 2020. Nick has previously gone skydiving and aims to go bungee jumping in the near future.

Jasmine Nunez

Jasmine’s daily goal is to empower emerging student leaders. Her previous leadership experience, in various professional development nonprofits, shaped that goal. As someone who is impact-driven, Jasmine took the initiative in her Junior year to co-found Diverse Influencers (DI). She currently amplifies her passions and goals through DI. Recently, Jasmine completed her 4th internship as an undergraduate - which included a Corporate Diversity internship at NBCUniversal. The experience fully sparked her enthusiasm for diversity & inclusion initiatives. In a few weeks, Jasmine will be graduating from Queens College in Media Studies. Her dream career is to be a Campus Diversity Recruiter. In her free time, she likes to listen to podcasts and learn how to cook better.

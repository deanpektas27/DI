Program Overview

Workshops are conducted by 5 different business and tech professionals. Students will learn how to develop their soft and hard skills to be prepared for future internships and networking events.

Our workshops always introduce students to brand new professionals, so students can rapidly expand their network and learn about different career paths.

We ensure the environment of our workshops is a space for students to make mistakes and learn to better themselves. Students arenâ€™t expected to know everything, as they are still in their early college careers. We only ask for students to show effort.

In addition to our workshops, Fellows receive a mentor to provide them with one-on-one career coaching. Fellows will meet with their mentor at least once a month throughout the duration of the year long program.

Benefits

    • Rapidly expand your professional network
    • Discover what career path is right for you
    • Meet students across different CUNY's
    • Receive a mentor to provide you with one-on-one career coaching

Eligibility

Requirements for Freshmen:

    • Attending a CUNY college (two-year or four year)
    • Will have no more than 30 credits by May 22, 2020*
    • Interest in pursuing a career in a business or tech related field (all majors accepted)
    • Available to attend bi-weekly Saturday workshops for the Fall and Spring semesters

Requirements for Sophomores:

    • Attending a CUNY college (two-year or four year)
    • Will have no more than 60 credits by May 22, 2020*
    • Interest in pursuing a career in a business or tech related field (all majors accepted)
    • Available to attend bi-weekly Saturday workshops for the Fall and spring semesters
    • Minimum GPA of 3.2

Applications open in July!

* We do not count college credits earned in high school count towards your credits earned in college.
